Why ICT Suits Me

I have chosen to study ICT because I have been very intrigued by technology all my life. This curiosity used to be very surface level, until I discovered what is behind the surface level, the code. I took a CS class in High School and it was one of the classes where I really enjoyed myself, so I chose to pursue it.

I can see a future in the ICT field because I like staying up to date. ICT will most certainly be involved in the evolution of the future and I want to be a part of that. I feel this way because of the evolution there has already been in such a short amount of time. An article from the European Patent Office shows evidence of the continuous boom in the tech industry. This boom does not seem to be stopping any time soon. Especially with artificial intelligence becoming so much more prominent in the world of today, there will be plenty of jobs in the tech industry for many years to come. In the time table below, I will show you how the ICT field has evolved since 1900 and will keep evolving, according to evidence from Brittanica:

  • 1901: The first Radio
  • 1927: The first Television
  • 1937: The first Computer
  • 1957: The first artificial sattelite
  • 1974: The introduction to the Internet
  • 2017: The introduction of Artificial intelligence
