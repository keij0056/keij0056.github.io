My Study Choice

I chose this study programme because I am working towards becoming a software engineer. Another reason for choosing this program was the fact that it is close to my family in the Netherlands so there is no housing problem. Lastly, I chose this program because it is focused solely on ICT, and there are no general classes, like other languages, or math classes as seen in most universities in the United States. I feel like this will help me dedicate my all to becoming the best I can be in the IT program. I want to do this because IT is such a developing world that I do not want to miss out on. This is because I like staying up to date. I feel if I do not stay up to date then the world will move on without me, which I do not want.
