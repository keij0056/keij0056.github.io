Personal SWOT Analysis

Strength

I would say that my biggest strength is my eagerness to learn. Especially in an always evolving field like IT, where you never stop learning, this will be a major help.

Weakness

I am a pretty big overthinker, this is a major weakness because it keeps me from doing my best work possible without the worry of "Will it be good enough?"

Opportunities

I can turn my strength into a major opportunity for maintaining a succesful career because I will not get left in the dust. As soon as there is something new for me to improve on, I will get right on that. My weakness can also be seen as an opportunity because the overthinking of "Is this good enough?" can be turned into a prime example of growth mindset.

Threats

The biggest obstacle I can face as a student is to doubt myself. I must not let myself get into the mindset that this will never work out. Instead, I should find different ways to resolve the issue at hand.
