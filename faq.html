How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

In order to print a document from your laptop at HZ, you must go to the HZ print page, log in with your HZ account, select what you would like to print, confirm what you would like to print, go to a printer, scan your student card, then just wait till it is done printing.

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

In order to scan a document at HZ, find the nearest printer (they can be found at multiple locations around the building. Then, scan the document you would like to scan, and this will allow you to send the pdf file to your HZ e-mail address. After that, you can do whatever you want with this document, since it is now on your laptop.

How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event on Friday 23/09) on the HZ web shop?

In order to buy something from HZ webshop, go to webshop.hz.nl. There you can login with your HZ email, and then browse around the website. Once you have found what you want to buy, add it to your shopping cart and follow the payment directions.

Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID?

To find the latest information regarding coronavirus, go to the HZ website. As you arrive at the home page, go to current affairs, and then information about the coronavirus. There you will find three different options, latest updates, Corona FAQ, and Are you infected? Click on the latest option, and there you will find directions on what to do if you are sick or show COVID symptoms.

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

You can book a project space by going to the HZ Self Service Portal, then select your location and time slot, the amount of people who will be in the room, and then book the room.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

The best place to park at HZ Middelburg is at the free parking lot at Poelendaelesingel 8. As you arrive at the lot, follow the HZ signs, and park in the designated spots. After parking here, the walk to HZ will be a few minutes (there is some construction ongoing so do not take the pedestrian tunnel). If needed, there is a handicap parking space right next to the actual HZ building. To enter HZ parking, you can either scan your student card, or ring the bell.
